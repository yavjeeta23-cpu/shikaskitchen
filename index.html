About Shika's Kitchen

Shika's Kitchen is a home-based culinary passion project, born from a love of authentic, hearty cooking. Every dish is prepared fresh each weekend in a home kitchen in Mauritius, using time-tested family recipes and the finest local ingredients.

The menu spans the rich multicultural food heritage of Mauritius — from fragrant Mauritian curries and Indian dals to Italian lasagnas, Chinese fried rice, and seasonal salads. No preservatives, no shortcuts.
