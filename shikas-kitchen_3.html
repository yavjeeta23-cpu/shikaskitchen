    About Shika's Kitchen

    Shika's Kitchen is a home-based weekend kitchen in Mauritius, where every dish is prepared fresh, in small batches, the way it would be cooked for family. From slow-simmered island curries and fragrant briyani to comforting pasta bakes and wok-tossed Mauritian-Chinese classics, everything is made with honest ingredients and a generous hand.
